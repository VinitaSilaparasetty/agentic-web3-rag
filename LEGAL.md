# Legal, Attribution, and Takedowns

This app is a developer search tool. Results show short excerpts (if permitted) and always link to the original documentation.

- We respect robots.txt and a per-domain policy defined in `configs/SOURCES.yaml`.
- Unknown domains default to **link-only** (no cached text).
- For CC-BY/compatible licenses we provide attribution and may show a short snippet or (when licensed) full text.
- For “All Rights Reserved” or restrictive ToS, results are **link-only** with a note that content is protected.

### Takedown / Opt-out
If you are a rightsholder and want your site removed or restricted:
1. Email: takedown@yourdomain.example
2. Include the domain(s) and example URLs.
3. We will remove cached snippets/vectors and add your domain to our denylist.

### Disclaimer
This project is provided for educational and developer productivity purposes. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by third-party docs publishers. Trademarks and names are used nominatively to identify sources.
